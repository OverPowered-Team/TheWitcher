The Witcher: A Bard's Tale is a Beat 'em up and Hack and Slash cooperative adventure game.
You will play the role of Geralt the Rivia and Yennefer of Vengerberg, on their quest to rescue Ciri.



The game will introduce a series of “relics”, objects that when grabbed, will improve one of the existing combos, making it stun, burn the enemies!
By shifting what combos do in each game, the player will be incentivized to use different combos, which in the long will traduce into knowledge of what each combo does. The player will end up mastering its character, each time being able to complete their adventure with faster times and better scores.
